In dashboard A, we see 2 charts and 1 KPI card.

The kpi card allows a viewer to easily recognize yearly sales.
A profit card will be added that allows users to easily recognize that as well.

The charts easily allow users
a.) visualize which months are most profitable
b.) visualize a breakdown of what categories sell the most by month.

Unsurprisingly, we notice sales trend upwards in the Winter months; likely due
to the gift-giving nature of them. We also note that a vast majority of the 
companies sales is coming from Office Supplies; a more agressive marketing campaign
tailored to the other categories may  be in order. That, or dropping other categories to save money.
